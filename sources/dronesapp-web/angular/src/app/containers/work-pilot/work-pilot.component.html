How It Works

  • Complete the drone pilot signup form, including your business information and a copy of your pilot/operator license and insurance information if you’re on an annual policy.

  • Once you’re approved, you can specify your preferences for types of work, industries, budget range, and (if you like) distance from your location. We’ll notify you of new job postings that match what you’re looking for. You can browse client job listings in any area and place bid proposals on the ones that interest you.
  • Once you’re hired, the client submits payment upfront into our secured holdings account. This gives assurance that you will be paid for your work.
  • When the payment has been made, you will be notified and OK to Fly will show up on the job status bar on your dashboard. Thanks to our secure payment platform, prompt payment for your work is always guaranteed!
