How It Works

Drones4Hire makes it quick and easy to find, hire, and pay the right pilot for your project’s needs — in just four simple steps.

  • Create a project listing that details the location, requirements, and completion date.
  • Licensed and insured drone pilots from our network submit all-inclusive bid proposals on your project. All bids are organized in a single report so you can quickly review rates, pilot ratings and reviews, pilot portfolios, drone and camera equipment, and more.

  • Once your project is completed, our platform makes it easy to view and download your images or data. After you’ve reviewed the work product, just release your payment and your pilot is paid immediately for their work.
