How It Works

Need a drone pilot for an event, 3D mapping, asset inspection, or thermal imaging project? Drones4Hire makes it easy to find one. Our network of highly qualified professional drone pilots will browse the project listings and submit a bid for your project. Select a pilot with the rates, experience, and availability that work for you. Once they’re hired, our hassle-free system lets you send messages and submit secure payments all in one place.
